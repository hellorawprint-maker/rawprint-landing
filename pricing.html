Common questions
What's the difference between this and therapy? +
Therapy is clinical, licensed, and designed to treat diagnosed conditions. Rawprint is a life design process — it's for people who are functioning but feel misaligned. We work with patterns, systems, and habits, not diagnoses. If your session suggests you'd benefit from clinical support, we'll tell you directly and help you find the right person.
How does the session actually work? +
One hour. Unscripted. You fill a short application form before — this helps calibrate the session, not screen you. The conversation goes wherever is honest: work, relationships, childhood, patterns, what you want but can't say out loud. After, we analyze the session and produce your report within 72 hours.
Can I pay in installments? +
Yes, for Blueprint and 90-Day packages. Blueprint: deposit confirms the session, first installment confirms the report delivery, second installment confirms community access. 90-Day: three installments across the 90 days. You never pay for something before it's delivered to you.
Is the session online or in person? +
Both options are available. In-person sessions are in Alexandria. Online sessions are via Google Meet or Zoom — same depth, same process. Most people choose based on comfort, not location.
Will my session be kept private? +
Full privacy. Your session content, report, and check-in data are never shared without explicit written consent. If we ever use anonymized stories for content, we'll ask you directly — and you can decline with no impact on your service. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
What if it doesn't work for me? +
The deposit is non-refundable once your session slot is confirmed — it holds a real hour of time. If the session genuinely didn't deliver what it promised, we'll have an honest conversation about it. We'd rather you leave with something useful than leave at all.
