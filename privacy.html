The short version: We collect only what we need to run your session and build your Blueprint. We never sell your data. We never share your story without your explicit permission. You can ask us to delete everything at any time.

Who we are

Rawprint is a life design service based in Alexandria, Egypt, operated as a registered freelance practice. We help people understand their psychological patterns and build personalized life systems. We are not a medical service, a therapy clinic, or a diagnostic service.

Contact: hellorawprint@gmail.com · 01224921659

What information we collect

We collect information you provide directly to us through:

  • The application form — your name or preferred name, age range, location, what you're working on, and what brings you to Rawprint.
  • The session itself — an audio recording of your conversation with us (with your explicit consent). This recording is used solely to generate your Blueprint Report and is deleted within 30 days of report delivery.
  • Weekly check-in forms — your self-reported data on habits, energy, mood, and reflections. Submitted voluntarily.
  • Contact information — your email address or WhatsApp number, used only to communicate with you about your session and follow-up.
  • Payment records — transaction confirmations via Instapay. We do not store your bank details.

What we do not collect

  • We do not use tracking cookies or analytics scripts on our forms or tools.
  • We do not use your data for advertising, targeting, or sale to any third party.
  • We do not collect your full name unless you choose to provide it.
  • We do not store payment card details.

How we use your information

  • To deliver your service — analyzing your session, generating your Blueprint Report, and providing follow-up.
  • To communicate with you — sending your report, follow-up emails, check-in reminders, and community updates.
  • To improve the service — identifying patterns in anonymized, aggregated data to improve our questions and frameworks.

Session recordings and reports

Your session is recorded with your consent at the start of the call. The recording is:

  • Used only to generate your Blueprint Report.
  • Not shared with anyone outside the Rawprint team (which, at this stage, is one person).
  • Deleted within 30 days of your report delivery unless you specifically request otherwise.

Your Blueprint Report is personal and confidential. It is never shared with any third party without your explicit written consent — including your family, partner, employer, or any other person.

Community and anonymized stories

If we ask to use your story as part of Rawprint's content (social media, website, podcast), we will:

  • Ask for your explicit written consent before using anything.
  • Remove all identifying details — name, location, specific workplace, relationship details, or anything that could identify you.
  • Send you the anonymized version for review and approval before it is published.
  • Stop using the content immediately if you withdraw consent, even after publication.

Participation in community content is always optional. It will never be a condition of receiving the service.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We may share minimal data with the following categories of service providers who help us operate:

  • Google (Forms, Meet, Drive) — for form collection and session calls. Google's privacy policy applies to their services.
  • Email providers (Brevo or similar) — for sending automated emails. We share only your email address and first name.
  • AI tools (Claude/ChatGPT) — we paste anonymized session transcripts into AI tools for analysis. We do not include your full name, contact details, or identifying information in these prompts.

We may be required to disclose information if compelled by Egyptian law or legal process. We will inform you of any such requirement unless legally prohibited from doing so.

Data storage and security

Your data is stored in Google Drive (for session files and reports) and in our email platform. Both use industry-standard encryption. We apply reasonable security practices appropriate to a small personal service.

We are a small operation and do not have the infrastructure of a large company. If you have specific security concerns, please contact us directly.

Your rights

Under Egypt's Personal Data Protection Law (Law No. 151 of 2020), you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate information.
  • Deletion — ask us to delete your data. We will do so within 14 days and confirm in writing.
  • Withdrawal of consent — withdraw consent for content use, recording, or community participation at any time.
  • Portability — request your data in a readable format (PDF or text file).

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hellorawprint@gmail.com · 01224921659 with the subject line "Data Request." We will respond within 7 working days.

Children and minors

Rawprint is intended for people aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has submitted an application, please contact us immediately.
